Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our web development services and pricing.

How long does it take to build a website?

Most projects take 2-6 weeks depending on complexity. Basic websites can be completed in 2-3 weeks, while more complex sites with e-commerce functionality may take 4-6 weeks.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes, we typically require a 50% deposit to begin work, with the remaining balance due upon completion. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments.

What if I need revisions?

We include two rounds of revisions in all our packages. Additional revisions can be accommodated at an hourly rate of $50/hour.

Do you provide website maintenance?

Yes, we offer ongoing maintenance plans to keep your website secure, up-to-date, and running smoothly. Plans start at $50/month.
